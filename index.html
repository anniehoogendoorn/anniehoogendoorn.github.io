About me


I'm Annie, a web developer based in Portland, Oregon. In December 2015 I graduated from Epicodus, an intensive five-month coding bootcamp, where I learned the fundamentals of JavaScript and PHP. Currently I am a web developer at Sq1, a digital agency in Portland.

I’ve been interested in web development for years, but my passion grew while working for a digital marketing agency in Amsterdam. Initially I started at the company as a photo editor but eventually found my way to a Web Designer position. When I realized that coding and problem solving were my favorite parts of the job, I decided to apply to Epicodus. I love the endless possibilities of the web and how it helps us to share information and improve our lives. I'm also really into new music, photography, non-fiction, documentaries, podcasts, hiking and traveling.
